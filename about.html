About us!

We are a company that has been in the business of creating premium chess sets, boards, and merchandise since 1845. For over a century, we have dedicated ourselves to crafting the highest quality chess products that have become synonymous with luxury and sophistication. Our love for the game of chess is what drives us to create exceptional products that are not only visually stunning but also durable and functional. Each piece is carefully crafted by skilled artisans using only the finest materials, ensuring that every game played with our products is an unforgettable experience. But our passion for chess extends beyond just creating beautiful sets. We strive to promote and celebrate the game's rich history and culture through our merchandise, which includes chess-themed clothing, accessories, and decor. We believe that chess is more than just a game, it's a way of life, and we are proud to be a part of the community that cherishes it. As we continue to grow and evolve, we remain committed to upholding our legacy of excellence and innovation. We invite you to join us in celebrating the timeless game of chess with our exquisite products and merchandise.
